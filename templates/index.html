About Altamura

A city carved
in stone and time.

Altamura is a city of the Alta Murgia, known for its Cathedral commissioned by Frederick II, the karst landscape of the Pulo di Altamura and its famous Pane di Altamura DOP. The city blends history, gastronomy and breathtaking landscapes.

  • Cathedral commissioned by Frederick II
  • Pane di Altamura — DOP protected product
  • Pulo di Altamura — karst depression
  • Altamura Man — Neanderthal remains from Lamalunga Cave
